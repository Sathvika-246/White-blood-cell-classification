WBC Types We Classify

🔬

Neutrophils

40-60% of WBCs. First responders to bacterial infections.

🛡️

Lymphocytes

20-40% of WBCs. Key to adaptive immunity and antibody production.

🦠

Monocytes

2-8% of WBCs. Largest WBCs that become macrophages.

⚡

Eosinophils

1-4% of WBCs. Combat parasitic infections and allergies.
