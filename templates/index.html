Understanding White Blood Cells

White blood cells are the guardians of your immune system. Learn about the different types.

🔬

Neutrophils

40-60% of WBCs

First responders to bacterial infections. They engulf and destroy harmful bacteria.

🛡️

Lymphocytes

20-40% of WBCs

Produce antibodies and destroy virus-infected cells. Key to adaptive immunity.

🦠

Monocytes

2-8% of WBCs

Largest white blood cells. Transform into macrophages to clean up debris.

⚡

Eosinophils

1-4% of WBCs

Combat parasitic infections and play a role in allergic reactions.
